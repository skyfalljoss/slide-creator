2Executive Summary

Problem: Citi bankers and staff spend 30-40% of pitch preparation time formatting slides, reconciling brand standards, and manually redacting non-compliant language. Current AI tools are blocked or lack enterprise controls, creating shadow IT risk.

Solution – SlideForge: A secure, internal web application that uses Google Gemini 1.5 Pro via Vertex AI to generate first-draft PowerPoint decks from a natural language prompt, structured data upload, or meeting notes. All output is rendered through the Citi Template Engine using python-pptx, ensuring 100% brand compliance.

Key Differentiation: Unlike public tools, SlideForge enforces Citi Blue (#056DAE) master layouts, auto-applies disclaimers, blocks client MNPI from model training, and logs every generation for audit. No prompts or content are retained beyond 30 minutes.

3Goals and Non-Goals

IN SCOPE – Goals

  • Reduce deck creation time by >60% for standard pitches
  • Generate client-ready PPTX in Citi master template automatically
  • Enforce brand, legal disclaimers, and accessibility (WCAG AA)
  • Support prompt-to-deck and data-to-chart workflows
  • Meet Citi security: no model training, US data residency, full audit

OUT OF SCOPE – Non-Goals

  • Real-time collaborative editing (use PowerPoint after export)
  • Image generation or video creation
  • Integration with external LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude)
  • Automated email distribution to clients
  • Long-term content library or slide repository (V2)

10Data Flow and Privacy – Banking Compliance

  • ✓No Training: Contract with Google ensures Citi data is NOT used to train Gemini models.
  • ✓Data Residency: Vertex AI endpoints locked to us-central1 and us-east4.
  • ✓Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest, CMEK keys rotated 90 days.
  • ✓Ephemeral: Prompts, files, and generated PPTX auto-deleted after 30 minutes.
  • ✓Audit: Every call logged to Splunk (user, time, model, tokens, deck type). Content never logged.
